Definition 1.6.7. Annual Percentage Yield.

The annual percentage yield (APY) of an investment (also called the effective rate) gives the return on investment in one year. APY for discretely compounding interest is calculated with the formula
\begin{equation*} APY_D = \left[\lrpar{1 + \dfrac{r}{n}}^n -1\right]\cdot 100\% \end{equation*}
