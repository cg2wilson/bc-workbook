Definition 1.6.1. Future Value/Present Value.

The future value of an investment/loan at time \(t\) is the sum of the prevent value and all accumulated interest; this is denoted \(F\) or \(FV\text{.}\) The present value, denoted \(F(0) = P\) (principal) is the value `'today'', or at \(t = 0\text{.}\)
